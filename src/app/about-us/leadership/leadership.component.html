Morayo Sanni

Morayo Sanni is the Managing Director at MoneyMarque Finance.

His years of experience as a Relationship Manager at Page Financials, Lagos City Manager at Transnet, have earned him an outstanding knowledge of sales and business development.

During his time at Transnet, Morayo deployed his leadership and business transformation skills leading the sales and business development team towards ensuring that Palmcredit became the most downloaded loan application.

He holds a BSc in Chemical Engineering from Landmark University and a Post Graduate Diploma in Business Management and Leadership from the Prestigious London School of International Business (LSIB). He is currently running an MBA program at the University of Sunderland, England.

Morayo is a resilient hardworking professional, responsible for providing strategic and operational leadership at MoneyMarque Finance

Aroyewun Omoniyi

With over 12 years of experience in banking operations, Aroyewun Omoniyi is the current Director of Risk at MoneyMarque Finance.

He began his career at Rosabon as a credit control officer. Thereafter, moved to Renmoney Microfinance Bank, Transnet Financials, and until recently, SLS Microfinance bank, where he served as the Head of Risk.

Omoniyi brings to play at MoneyMarque Finance, his ability to develop risk management frameworks that ensure healthy lending and compliance with existing regulatory policies.

He is a member of the Compliance Institute of Nigeria (CIN) and also has an MBA in view from Ado Bayero University.

Oluwatobi Olanipekun

Oluwatobi Olanipekun is a non-executive director at MoneyMarque Finance. She has over 20 years of experience in strategy formulation and execution, business process transformation, financial management, etc.

Tobi worked in PWC for over 14 years where she managed project teams of 3 to 15 consultants in the delivery of a wide range of business transformation and organization development projects for several top-tier public and private sector clients in Nigeria.

She also gained vast experience in the public sector having worked with clients on monitoring and evaluation, impact assessments, performance review, and change management projects.

Tobi holds a Master’s Degree in Finance with Distinction from the University of Leicester, United Kingdom. She is a fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria, a member of the Association of Investment Advisers and Portfolio Managers, a member of the chartered institute of stockbrokers.

She has a lot of publications on the pension industry from Micro-Pension – The New Frontier: Business Day (Wednesday 10 May 2017), The Nigerian Pension Industry – Overcoming Post-Reform Challenges (November 2016), The Nigerian Pension Industry – Overcoming Post-Reform Challenges: Business Day (Thursday 27 October 2016), etc.

Mayowa Sanni

Mayowa Sanni is currently the Executive Director of Finance at MoneyMarque Finance.

With over 8 years of experience in financial planning and monitoring, he is a relevant player in driving financial inclusion through his solid grasp on the fundamentals of budgeting, analysis, compliance, risk management, and other accounting principles.

Before MoneyMarque, he gained experience in effectively managing financial operations from various financial institutions such as Skye bank, Pagatech Ltd, Accion Microfinance Bank, Access Bank, and Stanbic IBTC bank.

Mayowa holds A Diploma in Operations Management, a B.Sc. in Banking and Finance from Covenant University, an M.Sc. in Risk Management and Insurance from the University of Lagos.

He also holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Strategic Management and Leadership from the renowned London School of International Business (LSIB) and an MBA in view from the University of Sunderland, England.
